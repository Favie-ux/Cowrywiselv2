About Cowrywise

Cowrywise is a financial technology brand that aims to digitize wealth management for Africans. They are focused on building a savings and investment culture among the growing population of underserved African middle class and millennials. With a team of skilled individuals, Cowrywise offers a range of products and services to help users achieve their financial goals.


For personal finance, they provide access to financial tools and guides, allowing users to save and earn better interests than traditional banks. They also offer the opportunity to build a global portfolio in one app, enabling users to invest their money wisely. Additionally, Cowrywise offers growth tools that estimate interests and help users understand their risk appetite.


For businesses, Cowrywise offers the Business Sprout feature, which allows users to put their business idle cash to work. They also provide investment services for developers to embed in their apps, offering their users investment opportunities. As a licensed fund manager, Cowrywise prioritizes customer satisfaction and ensures open and honest communication.


They value collaboration, innovation, and personal growth, and strive to create simple yet powerful experiences for their customers. Overall, Cowrywise is committed to providing Africans with accessible and efficient wealth management solutions, empowering them to achieve their financial goals
